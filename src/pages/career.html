Our values

Supporting each other

If your colleague doesn't know how to do something - explain, help, tell, teach. If you don't know something - just ask. You will be explained, helped, taught.

Self-development

We are in favour of you growing as a specialist in our company. Do you want to learn, develop, take part in conferences? Betatransfer will support you! Don't forget to invite us with you, because most likely you won't be the only one interested.

Initiative

Do you see what is not working properly? Do you know how the service could be improved? Is our company not doing something and you feel like you should? Tell me! Initiative is not punishable, but encouraged. Of course, if it's a top idea.

Liberty

We know you have a personal life outside of work. We all do. And that's great. We don't infringe on personal life. We are in favour of employees having a busy life that does not interfere with work, and we want to see bright personalities in the team.
