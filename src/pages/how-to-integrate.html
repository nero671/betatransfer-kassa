Integration steps

  1. Step

    Leave an request and communicate with a manager

    After receiving your request you are contacted by a manager who provides you with all the necessary information about future cooperation.

  2. Step

    Create an account on the Betatransfer Kassa

    After receiving instructions from the manager, you need to go through a simple registration procedure to access the service.

  3. Step

    Pass the moderation

    Our security service will check whether your project meets the requirements of the service and payment systems. You can find the requirements on our website.

  4. Step

    Accept payments

    Working with the online service on individual terms, taking into account the specifics of your merchant. Integration can take less than an hour. You will start accepting payments the same day.
